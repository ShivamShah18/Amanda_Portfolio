L'Oréal Brandstorm: Multiverse

Brand Strategy & Creative Direction | Immersive Experience Design

Project Overview

L'Oréal Brandstorm is a prestigious global innovation competition that challenges emerging talent to develop breakthrough brand concepts. I served as Brand Strategist and Creative Director, leading the conceptualization and execution of a comprehensive brand identity centered around the "Multiverse" concept.

My role was to create a compelling brand narrative that connected contemporary wellness trends, beauty innovation, and immersive experience design into a cohesive strategic brand platform.

My Role & Responsibilities

  • Brand Strategist: Developed the strategic positioning, market analysis, and consumer insights that informed the brand concept
  • Creative Director: Led the visual identity development, including logo design and brand narrative creation
  • Immersive Experience Designer: Conceptualized how the brand would create multi-sensory, engaging brand experiences
  • Storyteller: Crafted the "Multiverse" narrative that forms the foundation of the brand identity

Key Project Elements

Brand Concept: "The Multiverse"

The Multiverse concept speaks to the multifaceted nature of modern beauty and wellness. It represents how individuals exist in multiple worlds—professional, personal, aspirational—and how beauty and wellness are integral to navigating and thriving in each dimension.

Strategic Positioning

  • Target Audience: Forward-thinking, diverse, wellness-conscious consumers
  • Brand Promise: Empowering individuals to be their best selves across all dimensions of life
  • Key Differentiator: Holistic approach to beauty that bridges wellness, innovation, and personal expression

Visual Identity & Logo Design

Developed a distinctive visual identity that captures the essence of the Multiverse concept through:

  • Geometric, interconnected design language representing multiple dimensions
  • Color palette that balances sophistication with vibrancy
  • Logo that is both modern and meaningful, telling the brand story at a glance
  • Typography and design system that supports cohesive brand expression across channels

Immersive Experience Design

Beyond traditional branding, I designed immersive experiences that bring the Multiverse concept to life:

  • Interactive brand activations that engage multiple senses
  • Narrative journeys that guide consumers through the brand story
  • Digital and physical touchpoints that create cohesive brand worlds
  • Community engagement strategies that build brand loyalty and advocacy

Outcomes & Impact

The L'Oréal Brandstorm project demonstrated my ability to:

  • Develop strategic brand concepts grounded in consumer insights and market trends
  • Create visually compelling identities that tell meaningful stories
  • Lead cross-functional teams in executing complex creative projects
  • Design immersive experiences that engage and inspire
  • Communicate complex strategic ideas through compelling visual narratives

Skills & Tools Used

  • Brand Strategy & Positioning
  • Logo & Visual Identity Design
  • Adobe Creative Suite (Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop)
  • Figma for Design Systems
  • Strategic Storytelling & Narrative Development
  • Experience Design & Journey Mapping
  • Presentation & Communication Design

Key Learnings

This project reinforced my belief that great brands are built at the intersection of strategy, creativity, and human-centered design. The most successful brands tell stories that resonate with audiences and inspire them to be part of something larger than themselves. The "Multiverse" concept succeeded because it spoke to the diverse, multidimensional nature of modern life while offering a cohesive brand promise.
